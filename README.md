# Core & Main (core-and-main)

Core & Main is a leading specialty distributor of water, wastewater, storm drainage, and fire protection products and related services to municipalities, contractors, and engineers across the United States. As a Fortune 500 company, Core & Main operates more than 320 branch locations and provides products and services for water infrastructure, geosynthetics, fusible HDPE pipe, smart metering, fire protection, and erosion control.
